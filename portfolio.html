Website Design |
Artist Portfolio

This section was based on a brief to design any artist portfolio. My portfolio site was based on German, Morrocan artist by the name of Tasnim Baghdadi, based in Switzerland.
I am a huge fan of her work and am always inspired by her facination for both traditional and modern art. Her work is metaphorical and communicated in a very simplistic style. This played a major role in how I chose to create the layout for this UI. The idea was to keep the design simple, allowing the artworks to breathe. In terms of color, I was inspired by a common thread in her collections, of black and white along with shades of grey and red to add some variation.

Travel Blog

This section was based on a brief to design a travel blog, which I then based mine on a couple who travels all over and shares their experiences with the world.

The idea was to keep the design clean and simplistic, creating an easy user experience. I chose a theme of colors that to me, resembles nature. Colors such as green, orange- and then neutral colors such as black and white to create a balance.
